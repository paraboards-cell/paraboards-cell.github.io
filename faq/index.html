Frequently asked questions

What is ParaBoards?

ParaBoards is a modular mobility system built around The Chariot — a high-spec manual wheelchair designed from the start to accept power attachments. The Original ParaBoards (a power skateboard) and the ParaScooter connect to The Chariot and give you electric around-town capability. The Battery Box powers both. Every component uses standardized parts. You can fix it yourself and source parts from common vendors.

What does it cost?

Target pricing: The Chariot approximately $1,000. Each propulsion attachment approximately $1,000. Battery Box approximately $1,000. A fully powered single-attachment setup runs approximately $3,000 projected. The full system — both propulsion attachments plus Battery Box plus Chariot — runs approximately $4,000 projected. These are target figures, not final prices. See the pricing page for the full breakdown.

Do I need to buy the whole system?

No. The Chariot alone is a capable everyday manual chair. You start with what you need. Attachments are separate purchases you can add later. You can own one propulsion attachment or both — the system is designed to support either choice.

Where do I get replacement parts?

From common vendors — not from us. That's a design requirement, not a policy choice. Standardized parts that you can source independently are how we ensure you actually own the thing you bought. Specific parts lists and recommended sources will be published with the product documentation.

What happens if ParaBoards LLC closes?

Your rig still works. Every component uses standardized parts available from common vendors. You don't source parts through ParaBoards. If we close tomorrow, nothing about your ability to maintain and use the system changes. This is explicit design intent — not a promise about a hypothetical, but a functional property of the system.

What is a founding contribution?

A founding contribution of $150 lets you support the build and hold a place in the production queue. It is not a purchase. It is not a charitable donation and is not tax-deductible. It does not guarantee a product, a delivery date, or a specific price. It is a supporter pledge that gets credited toward your order if and when a product ships to you. If ParaBoards is unable to fulfill orders for any reason, founding contributions are refunded in full. Outside of that, they are non-refundable. Questions? Email us.

When can I buy one?

Nothing is for sale yet. All products are in development. Sign up below and we'll reach out when there's something real to share — not before.

Is this for wheelchair users only?

The Chariot is a wheelchair. If you use a wheelchair or want a capable modular chair with power options, it's for you. The system is designed to function well and be repairable by whoever owns it. It isn't designed for a category of person — it's designed for the task.
