How we got to these numbers

Full pricing rationale — parts cost breakdown, manufacturing targets, comparison context — will be published on this page. The short version: electric skateboard and scooter components at consumer scale are not expensive. The price you pay for a comparable mobility device is what it costs when volume is low, margins are structured for a different market, and nobody has optimized for the person paying the bill.

We have. These numbers reflect what we think is achievable at viable production volumes with standardized parts. They may change. We'll tell you if they do and why.
