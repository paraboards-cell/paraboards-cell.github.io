The Chariot

The chair the rest of the system is built around.

Every mobility device has a chair at the center. Ours was designed from the start to do more than sit. The Chariot ships as a high-spec manual chair. Every ParaBoards attachment connects to it. Attachments work with The Chariot because it was built for this.

What it is

The Chariot is a rollable seat platform and the base of the ParaBoards system. It ships with the castor attachment as standard — which makes it a capable, everyday rollable seat out of the box. When you're ready to add power, you connect a propulsion attachment and a Battery Box. That's the whole mechanism.

Parts are standardized and available from common vendors. You can service it yourself. If something breaks, you order the part — not through us. The goal is a chair you actually own, not one you're renting back from a service chain.
